About Us

Banadir Zone School

If you decide to study in Somalia, you’ll find plenty of opportunities to explore different Schools, Models, sights and general adventures in this huge and diverse Country. A total of 18 Schools are featured in the QS Somalia Secondary and Primary Schools Rankings® 2018, with the majority (nine) of these found in Mogadishu. The outstanding academic programs and the exceptional student experience we proffer have aided us to receive the highest accolade of all; Banadir Zone is ranked as the No.1 school in Mogadishu – Banadir Region.
